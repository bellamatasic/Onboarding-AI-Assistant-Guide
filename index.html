Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if a lead wants to talk to a real person?

A: The AI seamlessly transfers to your front desk or books an appointment for a callback.

Q: Can we customize what the AI says?

A: Absolutely. Scripts, tone, and personality are fully customizable during onboarding.

Q: What happens if our POS goes down?

A: The system continues calling and booking. We'll sync data once your POS is back online.

Q: Do we need to change our current workflows?

A: Minimal changes. Your GM just needs to mirror AI bookings into your POS daily.

Q: Can we pause or adjust calling hours?

A: Yes. You control blackout hours, preferred times, and call volume.

Q: What if we want to make changes after launch?

A: We optimize continuously. Monthly reviews allow for script updates, tone adjustments, and strategy changes.
